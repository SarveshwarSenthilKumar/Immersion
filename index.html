IMMERSION:
The Single Story of a Developer
NOW FILMING · A CINEMATIC DISRUPTION
A non-linear odyssey through the single story of a developer filled with ups and downs of code, and the ghosts of creation and ambition. IMMERSION weaves together the intensity and technicality of The Social Network, the moral quandaries of Frankenstein, and the technological dilemmas of Jurassic Park. Witness a young developer’s rise from late-night commits to an empire of an alternate universe — and the singular story that haunts the architecture of modern dreams.

“Everything comes from something before.” — Northrop Frye. A satire on ambition through the ethics of innovation and the dangers of technology. Shot in Toronto & the digital sprawl.
  • Directed by Sarveshwar Senthil Kumar
  • Genre Techno-Psychological Drama / Essay Film
  • Runtime TBA (Director's Cut)
  • Premiere Fall 2026 — ONLINE
  • Languages English, with fragments of code & silence
